A Sip of Soul – Chai

What is Chai?

Chai is not just tea—it's an emotion wrapped in warmth, a soulful blend of spices like cardamom, ginger, and cinnamon brewed with milk and love. It's that cozy moment in the chaos, the soundtrack to heartfelt conversations, rainy days, and stolen glances. In every desi heart, chai is comfort, nostalgia, and a little bit of love in a cup. ☕❤️

Types of Chai

Masala Chai

Classic Indian tea brewed with spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.

Adrak Chai

Strong black tea infused with the warmth of fresh ginger.

Kashmiri Chai

Pink tea with salt, baking soda, and milk, often topped with almonds.

Sulaimani Chai

Light black tea with lemon, often served after meals for digestion.

Elaichi Chai

Fragrant tea flavored with crushed green cardamom pods.

Kulhad Chai

Masala tea served in traditional clay cups, adding a rustic aroma.

Masala Chai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions: Boil water with spices, add tea, then milk and sugar. Simmer for 2–3 minutes, strain, and enjoy!
